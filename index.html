About Fidelity Investments

Fidelity Investments is one of the largest and most diversified financial services companies in the world. Founded in 1946, Fidelity offers a wide range of services to individuals, businesses, and institutions, including investment management, retirement planning, brokerage services, and more. Fidelity.com provides an easy-to-use platform for managing your investments and planning for your financial future.

Our Services

Fidelity offers a wide range of services to help you achieve your financial goals:

  • Investment Management: Comprehensive portfolio management, mutual funds, ETFs, and more.
  • Retirement Planning: Tools and resources to help you plan for retirement, including IRAs, 401(k) rollovers, and retirement calculators.
  • Brokerage Services: Low-cost trading with access to stocks, bonds, options, and other investments.
  • Financial Planning: Access to certified financial planners who can help you create a personalized financial plan.
  • Tax-Advantaged Accounts: Learn about tax-efficient investing through accounts like HSAs, 529 plans, and tax-deferred retirement accounts.

Investment Options

Fidelity offers a variety of investment options to help you build a diversified portfolio:

  • Stocks: Invest in individual stocks across various sectors.
  • Mutual Funds: Choose from a wide range of actively managed and index funds.
  • ETFs: Exchange-traded funds that offer a low-cost, diversified option for investors.
  • Bonds: Invest in government and corporate bonds to help preserve capital.
  • Options Trading: Gain access to options contracts for advanced strategies.
